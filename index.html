About DreamAdvance

Dream Advance, a series of workshops on emerging technologies like Drone Tech, Aero-modeling, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, SpaceTech, Smart Agriculture, 3D Printing, IOT, and more! These workshops are designed by experts from IITs and NITs to boost up the thinking capabilities of students from Class 3 onwards. All of these workshops are perfectly aligned with the academic curriculum so they not just provide a hands- on experience of technology but also improve the scores of students. In that sense, while teaching you about Aeromodelling we are going to clear all the concepts related to Science and Maths as well.

Deliverables By Us

  • Faculty

    Expert mentors from IITs and NITs will provide guidance to the students for projects .

  • Projects Kits

    Specially curated projects/kits will be provided to the students for a better learning experience.

  • Equipments

    All the necessary equipment like machine components, glue guns, soldering irons will be provided.

  • Study material

    All the students will be provided with relevant study material about their projects.

Aero-modelling

Are you excited to design your own remote controlled Aircraft?? Then this workshop is completely for you.
There is a huge scope for Aero-modelling as a career option and it also enhances your creative skills.
Don’t forget that We will also be having flying sessions for your hand made aircrafts.
Course Outline: Introduction to Aerodynamics, designing various Aircrafts like Gliders, RC Planes, Fixed wings Plane, Ornithopters, Rubber Propelled planes and many more.

Drone Technology

Have you heard that in the next 5 years most of the deliveries are going to be done through drones?? If you have even a slight interest in flexing with your hand made quadcopter, then this workshop is completely for you.
We’ll ensure that you have the maximum out of this workshop and harness your interest in more creative activities.
So the time has come to join our Drone Tech course now and build your own drone.
Course Outline: aerodynamics principles, basics of flying drones, Frame building, drone calibration, quadcopters, hexacopters, octa-copters and much more.

Artificial Intelligence

Have you heard about the AI revolution??? You might have used ChatGPT or Gemini. How about creating your own AI tools or harnessing the power of generative AI. We have come up with a workshop on AI especially curated for school students.
So let's build something interesting by exploring the concepts of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
Course Outline: In this workshop you are going to explore various generative AI tools like ChatGPT, Sora, Bard, Claudia and much more. In addition you will also build an AI model that will be completely designed as per your usability.

Space Tech

ISRO has successfully reached the moon. Do you wonder about space technology or want to create your own rockets and missiles?
Here the Cograd has come up with an exciting workshop on Space Tech. We will also be doing a simulation based Mars mission.
Exploring space is always amusing. Don’t miss the chance to join this workshop. Enrol now and explore the universe.
Course Outline: Basics of Aerodynamics, types of fuels, simulation of space missions, rocket designs, fuel cells, satellite designing.
